Why Glow Cable Guard?

Engineered to enhance safety and minimise trip hazards, Glow Cable Guard ensures your EV charging cables are highly visible and robustly protected—without requiring any power source.

  • ✔ Glows in the dark for enhanced visibility
  • ✔ Reflective surface for quick detection
  • ✔ Protects cable from wear and tear
  • ✔ Weatherproof & durable
  • ✔ No electricity or batteries required
  • ✔ Available in multiple lengths
  • ✔ Easy, tool-free installation
